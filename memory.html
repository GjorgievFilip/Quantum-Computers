Quantum Memory:

                    Rules of the Game:
Quantum memory is a parody of memory, which puts it's own twists into the classic card game.
Unlike normal memory, quantum memory requires you to find three identical cards (qubits in our case) to reveal them.
However, this isn't the only thing that differentiates it from the original concept.
There are also additional powerups that exist, allowing you to utilize them to beat the game.

The game is designed to be a fun and interactive way to learn about quantum computing concepts, such as superposition and entanglement.
By playing the game, players can gain a better understanding of how quantum computers work and the unique properties of qubits.
