Quantum Memory:

Quantum memory is a parody of memory, which puts it's own twists into the classic card game. Unlike normal memory, quantum memory requires you to find three identical cards (qubits in our case) to reveal them. However, this isn't the only thing that differentiates it from the original concept. There are also additional powerups that exist, allowing you to utilize them to beat the game.
